    • Banh beo - Steamed Rice Cakes

    By Phúc Hoàng
    Published October 17, 2024

    Banh beo,a delightful specialty from Central Vietnam, is a beloved snack that captivates with its delicate presentation and unique flavors.

    These small, round rice cakes are made from a smooth rice flour batter, steamed to perfection, and often served in tiny bowls. Each cake is typically topped with savory ingredients such as minced shrimp, fried shallots, and fresh herbs, creating a harmonious blend of tastes and textures.

    Served with a side of sweet and tangy dipping sauce, banh beo offers a delightful balance of flavors that excites the palate. The light and fluffy texture of the rice cakes pairs perfectly with the rich toppings, while the sauce adds an extra layer of flavor.

    This dish is commonly enjoyed as a snack or appetizer and can be found in many local eateries and street food stalls. Banh beo reflects the artistry of Vietnamese cuisine, emphasizing the importance of fresh ingredients and the skillful preparation that makes this dish a true culinary delight.
